Our Story

Get-Up Charitable Trust

Embarking on a transformative journey, we orchestrate and champion remarkable initiatives that breathe hope into the lives of underprivileged children and youth spanning diverse corners of our globe.

Our Mission

To ensure every child & youth is enveloped in the support they need, at the moment they need it, with absolute certainty & care.

Our Vision

Creating a future where every young heart & soul receives instant support—ensuring no one is left feeling isolated.

Mahendran Annadurai

Founder Director

I’m Mahendran Annadurai, the heart behind Get-Up Charitable Trust. My mission is to uplift the youth through education and employment, transforming lives across underprivileged communities.

From building schools to creating job opportunities, we’re dedicated to nurturing dreams and fostering futures. At Get-Up, we believe in immediate action for mental health and well-being, ensuring no child feels alone. Join us in our commitment to hope and dignity at Get-Up Charitable Trust.
